Bio

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at University of California, Santa Barbara(UCSB). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Shiyu Chang. My research interest mainly lies in efficient and trustworthy AI, especially large language models.

Previously, I was a master student of the Data Lab at Rice University under the supervision of Prof. Xia (Ben) Hu, and I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science at Wuhan University under the supervision of Prof. Yaochu Jin.
